TYPES OF CARS

Different Types of Cars:

Sedans

Sedans are regular cars with four doors and a separate trunk. Theyre mainly used for everyday driving and are pretty comfortable. Most people use them for things like going to school or work.

Coupes

Coupes are usually two door cars that look more sporty than sedans. They dont have as much space, but they focus more on style and performance.

SUV's

SUVs are bigger cars with more space and higher ground clearance. Theyre good for families and can handle rougher roads. A lot of them also come with all wheel drive. SUVs come in all ranges from low end to expensive.

Roadsters

Roadsters are small cars with two seats and usually no fixed roof. Theyre made for fun driving, not really for carrying a lot of stuff.

Supercars

Supercars are very fast cars with crazy engines and cool designs. Theyre built more for speed than for everyday use. You usually dont see them often because theyre expensive.

Drag Cars

Drag cars are built for straight line racing. They accelerate really fast but arent made for turning or normal driving. Theyre only used on drag strips and have specialized tires.

F1 Cars

F1 cars are race cars used in Formula 1. Theyre extremely fast and built to perform as well as possible on tracks. Everything about them is designed for speed and control and the drivers need to be exteremely skilled.

Hypercars

Hypercars are the absolute top end of cars and come for prices anywhere upwards of 1 million. They are each built uniquely with high performance engines and are a superior version of a supercar. These are very rare to see on a road as very foew owners have them. Having money isnt enought to buy a hypercar, the person should also have an allocation for the car which requires previous purchase history with the comppany.
