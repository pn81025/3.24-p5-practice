A Brief Overview of the History of Cars

When They Were Invented

The first modern car was invented in 1886 by Karl Benz, but earlier experimental vehicles date back to the 18th century, The first modern practical car is widely known as the Benz Patent Motorwagen *Image in Gallery* It was built in 1885

How They are being Used Today

Today cars play a big part in people's daily lives. The main role they play is getting you from one place to another but cars serve other purposes too. A big part of cars is racing, theres many different types of racing: some being F1, Nascar, and IndyCar. Cars are also collected by car enthusiasts, everyone has a different taste and collects different types of cars *more info in next section*. There is a seperate car community where people get together with their cars and show them off. Overall cars have many different roles in society besides just getting you from one place to another.
